Hey there, I'm Farel! Techie at heart, Indonesian by birth, and high school student by day (juggling textbooks and code, of course). I love tinkering with tech, building personal projects that tickle my fancy, and just geeking out over all things innovative. Hit me up on X or Instagram if you wanna chat about the latest gadgets, share coding tips, or brainstorm some cool techy ideas!

My main workhorse is an ASUS A416MA laptop running Windows and Linux. I use KernelOS for its minimalism and ArchLinux for its customization options. On my POCO X3 NFC phone, I've flashed TWRP and installed the DerpFest custom ROM for enhanced performance and features. My home network is powered by a ZTE HG680-P router running OpenWrt, with a secondary Bolt BL201 router running ImmortalWrt for added redundancy and coverage.

I manage and host a diverse range of personal websites, including an interactive dashboard for my Fast Reverse Proxy (frp) project, a customizable OpenWrt and ImmortalWrt luci panel, a user-friendly ariang panel, a secure webdav storage solution, and this profile website where I share my thoughts and projects.
